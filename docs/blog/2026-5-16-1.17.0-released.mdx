Extension v1.17.0 has been released at a faster pace, as it includes important fixes to subtitle detection.

<!-- truncate -->

- [@ShanaryS](https://github.com/ShanaryS) added a word browser, which allows users of annotation features to explore their known words.
- [@jprostko](https://github.com/jprostko) fixed subtitle detection on Amazon Prime and UR Play. Huge shout out for their high-quality contributions to subtitle detection.
- [@rodrigo-suarezmajor](ttps://github.com/rodrigo-suarezmajor) added a very highly-requested feature - the ability to choose which subtitle tracks are affected by keyboard shortcuts and playback modes.

Read the full release notes for v1.17.0 [here](https://github.com/asbplayer/asbplayer/releases/tag/v1.17.0).
